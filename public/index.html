What We Do

Tom Felling is an executive search consultant specializing in assisting companies in achieving greater success through their most important resource; people.  Tom is regarded nationally as one of the most dedicated and insightful “search” firms in the search business and has achieved an impressive 98.5% success rate in finding the most ideal individuals for client companies.  Clients benefit from his high ethical standards, values, and integrity.   Mr. Felling speaks regularly at various business and educational forums on the topic of employment.
