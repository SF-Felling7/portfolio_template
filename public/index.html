What We Do

Felling Executive Search is a full-service, retainer-based executive search and business consulting firm.

People are our business. We take this very seriously.

Each assignment is unique and is tailored to the specific requirements of our client.

Our passion at Felling Executive Search is uncovering the right individual(s) for our clients. Thorough research and very effective listening set us apart and have earned us a leading position within the executive search industry. Our clients also benefit from our high ethical standards, values and integrity.

In order to deliver our unique focus, we are selective in the search assignments that we accept. Felling Executive Search undertakes each search as if it were for its own company. We persevere until we find the key person for our client, period.

We are very dedicated and responsive to providing the highest quality for our clients and candidates. We work very closely with our clients and are proud of the many successes we have had finding the right person. These searches are indeed very rewarding to us.

Through our passion, hard work and success we intend to continue to earn our clients' business.

Key people do indeed make the difference. Our successful searches continue to pay high dividends for our clients well into the future.
